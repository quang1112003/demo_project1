About Us

Welcome to The Vietnam School, the leading and definitive resource of The International School in Vietnam! Our powerful multichanted media platform including websites, print publications and school fairs showcases a range of great learning opportunities in Vietnam – a medium for both parents and provides schools to ultimately make informed decisions for future students.

Throughout this website, you will find the latest news and findings on essential components concerning international education in Asia. With our experienced contributors in the international education scene in Asia, coupled with our team of dedicated professionals, we strive to offer accurate and authoritative information to our readers on preschools, international and private schools, pre-university programs and learning centers in VietNam. Our goal? To equip our readers with relevant, practical information on the international schools VietNam has to offer.

Read about the latest updates, open days and offers from international schools in VietNam in our News section. Obtain insights into international curricula in VietNam, international schools fees, as well as other essential information and guides on choosing the right school for your child in our Features section.

Also, be sure to check out our School Finder tool, a comprehensive list of international schools in , in the form of an easy-to-use map. Refine your search further by selecting the international curriculum you are interested in, the level of education, boarding availability and your choice of location to find a suitable international school for your child.
